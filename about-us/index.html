about us.

ali: Traveling is my passion. I love the opportunity for discovery that it provides. I joke that most of the decisions I make in life are guided by the single desire to have good stories to tell over dinner. I am only half joking. After losing all I owned, I once hitchhiked from Costa Rica to Arizona. I’ve slept in the same room as a green mamba – which, upon discovery, I ate. After participating in a local ceremony for warriors in West Africa, the traditional healer assures me that I am bullet-proof. I also like warm milk with cinnamon before bed, laughing until I cry, dancing without inhibition (and better yet, in costume!), and picking scabs until they bleed.

adam: i love music. listening and making. especially with others. i ride my bike a lot. once i rode from london to budapest. people were friendly and cars didn’t run over me. i camped in parks and slept on stranger’s floors. i came back with the knowledge of how much i’d taken ranch dressing and obesity for granted. i have a phd in physics and i am a huge fan of wikipedia and npr.
